Reunion Giving

As part of a longstanding Brandeis tradition, we invite you to join your former classmates on the Donor Honor Roll by giving to Brandeis in honor of your reunion. When you support Brandeis, you support current students in enjoying the unique Brandeis experience that was meaningful to you, and aid them in empowering themselves. Donate now to a school, department, or program that made your experience memorable. We hope to see those of you celebrating reunion this year at Alumni Weekend!
